Responsibilities

Conseqta’s Functional UI/UX Designer is responsible for gathering and evaluating user requirements, in collaboration with product managers and engineers to design ideas using storyboards, process flows and sitemaps. UX/UI Designer combines creativity with an awareness of design elements, creates prototypes for new product ideas and tests those new ideas before implementation.

This role is also responsible for:

  • Conducting an ongoing user research
  • Designing graphic user interface elements, like menus, tabs, and widgets.
  • Optimizing existing user interface designs
  • Communicating with clients and business analysts teams to understand their business goals and objectives
  • Conducting user research and testing
  • Giving and receiving feedback
  • Developing wireframes and task flows based on user needs
  • Collaborating with Designers and Developers to create intuitive, user friendly software

Required Skills

  • Detail oriented and curious mindset
  • Effective sketching and prototyping
  • Knowledge of design tools such as Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, In-Design, and After Effects
  • Ability to balance and prioritize many diverse tasks at once
  • Understanding of project schedules
  • Self-motivator with ability to work as part of a team or independently with little supervision or direction

Desired Skills

  • Knowledge of Agile Software Development Lifecycle and Process Workflow
  • Understanding of HTML, CSS and other web technologies
  • Knowledge of user-centered design processes
  • Preparing and Performing usability studies

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree
  • Minimum of 1 year of experience working for/on a client site
  • Attention to detail coupled with effective analytical and problem-solving aptitude
