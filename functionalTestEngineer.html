Responsibilities

Conseqta’s Functional Test Engineer is responsible for Identifying and documenting functional problems and suggest resolutions. The role involves working with multiple engineering groups in an Agile environment to define, document, analyze, perform, and interpret tests for products, systems, components, or modifications. The Test Engineer also assists in the definition of internal quality control standards and the maintenance of reliability programs, also analyzes test cases and provide regular progress reports. Other duties include reviewing and providing analysis-to-test documentation, producing testing artifacts and summary test reports, assisting in the reviewing and analyzing verification methods and results.

Qualifications

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Excel in the ability to execute and document results of test cases
  • Detail oriented and curious mindset
  • Experience participating in and facilitating requirements gathering sessions
  • Strong command of the Microsoft and/or Apple Suite (Word, Powerpoint, Excel, and Visio, Pages, Numbers)
  • Ability to balance and prioritize many diverse tasks at once
  • Understanding of project schedules
  • Self-motivator with ability to work as part of a team or independently with little supervision or direction
  • Attention to detail coupled with effective analytical and problem-solving aptitude

Knowledge of Agile Software Development Lifecycle and Process Workflow
